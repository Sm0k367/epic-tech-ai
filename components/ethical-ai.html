Ethical AI & Transparency

Epic Tech AI is built with alignment, safety, and transparency at its core. We believe in responsible AI that empowers, not exploits.

  • ✅ All models are labeled with source and version
  • ✅ Generated content includes optional watermarking
  • ✅ Prompt history is stored locally, not on our servers
  • ✅ Stripe-gated content is protected but never surveilled
  • ✅ No biometric data or facial recognition is used

We are committed to transparency, auditability, and human-aligned outcomes in all AI systems we deploy.
